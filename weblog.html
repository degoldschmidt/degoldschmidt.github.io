Hi, I am Dennis Goldschmidt,


I am currectly a PhD student working in the Behavior & Metabolism Lab at the Champalimaud Neuroscience Programme in Lisbon, Portugal.

There I work on
  • building experimental systems for closed-loop video tracking,
  • genetically manipulating the neural circuitry of fly brains,
  • and developing computing pipelines for automatic data analysis & visualizations.
